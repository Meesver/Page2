About the Artist

Our cyanotype artist has been pushing the boundaries of this unique photographic printing process for over a decade. With a keen eye for composition and a deep understanding of the interplay between light and chemistry, they create hauntingly beautiful images that capture the essence of their subjects.

Inspired by nature and the human form, their work often features delicate silhouettes of flora and fauna, as well as ethereal portraits that seem to emerge from the deep blue of the cyanotype process.
